Emma Watson's Dress in the new Beauty and the Beast is Just so Yellow
Nov 4, 2016
Tweet

Inspired by BuzzFeed's article on Emma Watson's dress in the new live action Beauty and the Beast, I thought I'd take a stab at re-coloring her dress. I mean, it's just so yellow. The kind of yellow that makes me think of artificial flavoring in lemon popsicles. I tried a few variations. (Please be patient with them; they are rough, done in a hurry).

The original

This just doesn't look expensive.


Light meringue

A tint of white makes it more romantic, almost like a wedding dress.


Champagne

This champagne version is closer to how I picture how cartoon-Belle's dress would look like in real life.


Gold

A red undertone gives the dress a gold or butterscotch look. This would go nicely with Beast's blue coat.


Of course, it's too early to judge. The designers reportedly are still working on the dress so we might get something totally new come March.
