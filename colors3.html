1. Pick a Starting Color

As before, choose a color you like to get started.

2. Apply a Color Scheme

Choose one of four color schemes: complementary, triadic, split complementary, analogous.

Complementary Color Scheme

The complementary scheme uses colors 180 degrees apart. It's a very popular color scheme in logos.

Rotate the propellor to get your complementary color palette.
