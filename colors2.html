If you haven't yet, check out Part 1 of this series which introduced color wheels and 2-color and 3-color palettes for the web. In this tutorial we'll make a 4-color palette for the web.

As before, this tutorial will use a web-optimized pure color wheel with 24 colors.

1. Pick a Starting Color

Choose a color you like to get started.

2. Apply a Color Scheme

To get the rest of your colors decide on a color scheme. For four colors you have three color schemes to choose from: tetradic, square and analogous.

Tetradic

The tetradic color scheme is vibrant and balanced. It consists of a starting color and three colors that are 60 degrees, 180 degrees, and 240 degrees from the base.
