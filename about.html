About Me

User Experience Design professional with a background in Software Engineering. I specialize in interaction design, information architecture and data visualization.

For six years I wrote software as an Engineer, then spent eight years validating software as an Automation Engineer and QA Manager. Most recently, I spent four years designing software as a User Experience Designer.

I focus on the whole team, from Sales, to Dev to QA, and on how design best fits into the software life cycle. I want to design good products but also good processes for delivering them and evaluating their success.

I love teaching and mentoring computer science students. In 2013-14, I instructed a Girls Who Code club and mentored a Samsung Technovation Challenge team.

I currently teach Computer Science at The Dalton School.
