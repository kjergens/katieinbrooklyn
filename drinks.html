Have you ever seen a scene in a movie when guy sidles up to a bar and orders a whiskey "straight up"? We all have, and many of us has copied this drink order, even though it makes no sense!

When I learned that a "whiskey straight up" was a bogus order I got inspired to look up commonly overheard drink terminology and illustrate what they actually mean. Here they are in no particular order.
