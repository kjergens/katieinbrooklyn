Color Wheel For Coffee
Tue Jan 26, 2016
Tweet

Want to describe your coffee like a pro? You're in luck! The Specialty Coffee Association of America updated it's Coffee Taster's Flavor Wheel for the first time since the original in 1998.

It goes beyond sweet, sour, bitter and salty, inviting you to describe coffee as papery, vegatative, smoky or caramelized.

The updated wheel well has brighter hues and white font. Negative space in the sub-flavors allow them to breathe. The overall effect is an airiness lacking in the original.

Now let's see...One half-caff papery latte with a lime aftertaste, please!
