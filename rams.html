A Letter to St. Louis On Losing Their Football Team
Tue Jan 19, 2016
Tweet

Dear St. Louis,

Do not worry about losing your football team to Los Angeles. Many of the finest cities in the world don't have football teams.

The thing is, most people don't like football. This is your chance to get to know them. Throw a literary festival; host a hackathon; have a graffiti competition! Who knows who you'll meet and where the relationships will go?

It feels bleak now, St. Louis, but you have new friends waiting to meet you. They will come, not in spite of, but because you are a football-free city.

Sincerely,

A potential new friend
