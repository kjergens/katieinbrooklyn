If you haven't yet, check out How To Create a Color Scheme - Part 1: Four Colors. Stay here to learn about two and three color schemes.

1. Pick a Base Color

Choose a color to like to get started. Once again, I'm choosing golden-yellow, third from the top red.

2. Choose a Color Scheme

Then decide on one of three color schemes: triadic, split complementary, or complementary.

a. Triadic

Triadic schemes are fully balanced. They consist of a base color and two colors that are 120 degrees and 240 degrees apart from the base respectively. Burger King, Best Western and Fanta soft drink uses this scheme.
